Portal Overview

In today’s digital age, learning mathematics should go beyond formulas and memorization. It should be engaging, interactive, and meaningful. This project presents an innovative web-based platform designed to help students better understand algebra through visual storytelling and structured problem-solving.

At the heart of the platform is the idea that students learn best when they can see, create, and apply concepts. By combining the 5-step problem-solving process with a storyboarding approach, learners are guided step-by-step in solving real-life algebraic problems such as age, motion, geometry, work, and financial situations.
